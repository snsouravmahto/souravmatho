Sir Dorabji Tata Park

Also known as Modi Park, Sir Dorabji Tata Park is a must-visit in Jamshedpur. It is one of the best hangout places in Jamshedpur and a perfect spot to spend some quality time with your loved ones. The centre of attraction of the park is the Diamond Pavilion, a marvel in steel. To witness the vivid colours of the lighted Diamond Pavilion, make sure to visit the park in the evening.
You can also see a fountain, a rose garden, paved walkways, and a fitness station here. The aura and the entire layout of the park will leave you spell-bounded. Also, complete access to the park is free of cost.

Jubilee Park

Spread across 225 acres, Jubilee Park is one of the best places to explore in Jamshedpur. The spot is famous not only among tourists but also among the local people of the town. Many people visit there to go cycling or jogging, while others get there to have a relaxing picnic with their family or friends. As the park has a lot of different features, people tend to spend their entire day exploring the park.

The Tata Steel company gifted this park to the people of Jamshedpur in 1958. It was founded to commemorate the fiftieth anniversary of Jamshedpur.
